Decision Point: Which Approach Will You Use?

You've previously defined the main idea or purpose of your presentation: informing the employees about the details of the reorganization and emphasizing the benefits of the reorganization for both them and the company. Now it's time to decide whether you will use a direct approach or an indirect approach in your presentation.

As with other forms of communication, a direct approach arranges the content in your presentation to introduce your main point before the supporting details. An indirect approach would present the supporting information before you state your main point.

Having analyzed your audience, which approach would be most appropriate for your presentation?
